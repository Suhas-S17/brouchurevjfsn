About the Journal

Vivrtih Journal of Food Science and Clinical Nutrition is an online-only, independent, researcher-led, international journal. Our mission is to bridge the gap between fundamental research and practical applications by translating scientific discoveries into real-world solutions.

Commitment to Quality

We are dedicated to publishing high-quality, peer-reviewed scientific work. The journal operates without any institutional or commercial affiliation, ensuring transparency, editorial independence, and adherence to ethical publishing standards.

Inclusivity

While maintaining high academic standards, the journal encourages submissions from students, early-career researchers, and young professionals, supporting inclusivity and the professional growth of emerging scholars.

Call for Papers

We are pleased to announce the call for papers for our inaugural issue, starting from January 2026. Submissions are now open, and we invite researchers, students, early-career professionals, and practitioners worldwide to contribute high-quality work aligned with our scope. Submissions are open for all.

We welcome a broad range of submissions, including original research, replication studies, and rigorously conducted null findings. Every valid investigation, regardless of outcome, provides valuable insights that advance the scientific community.

Articles Accepted

Original research, Review articles, Mini reviews, Short Reports, Case studies, Perspectives, Opinion Papers, Commentaries, Letter to editor.

Scope & Focus Areas

We welcome submissions across the entire spectrum of food science and nutrition:

  • Food Science: Chemistry, Sensory Analysis, Quality Control, Food Engineering, Processing, etc.
  • Clinical Nutrition: Therapeutic Dietetics, Nutritional Interventions, Personalised Nutrition, Disease Management, etc.
  • Nutrition & Health: Epidemiology, Public Health Nutrition, Dietary Assessments, etc.
  • Food Technology: Safety, Preservation, Packaging, Novel Production Systems, etc.
