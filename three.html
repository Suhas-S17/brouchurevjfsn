About the Journal

Vivrtih Journal of Food Science and Clinical Nutrition is an independent, researcher-led international journal dedicated to publishing high-quality scientific work.

Our Mission

To bridge the gap between fundamental research and real-world applications. We translate scientific discoveries into practical solutions for health, nutrition, and technology challenges.

Standards

We adhere to the UGC Suggestive Parameters for peer-reviewed journals. Operating without commercial affiliation ensures editorial integrity and unbiased decision-making.

Scope & Focus Areas

We welcome submissions across the entire spectrum of food science:

Food Science

Chemistry, Sensory Analysis, Quality Control, Food Engineering, and Processing.

Clinical Nutrition

Therapeutic Dietetics, Interventions, Personalized Nutrition, and Disease Management.

Nutrition & Health

Epidemiology, Public Health Nutrition, Dietary Assessments, and health impacts.

Food Technology

Safety, Preservation, Packaging, and Novel Production Systems.
