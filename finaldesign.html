About the Journal

Vivrtih Journal of Food Science and Clinical Nutrition is an online only, independent, researcher-led international journal. Our mission is to bridge the gap between fundamental research and real-world applications by translating scientific discoveries into practical solutions.

Commitment to Quality

We are dedicated to publishing high-quality, peer-reviewed scientific work. The journal operates without institutional or commercial affiliation, ensuring complete transparency and editorial integrity.

Inclusivity

While maintaining high academic standards, the journal also encourages submissions from students, early-career researchers, and young professionals, fostering inclusivity and the growth of emerging scholars.

For more information, kindly visit the journal website: www.vivrtihjournal.com

ISSN registration is in progress.

Scope & Focus Areas

We welcome submissions across the entire spectrum of food science and nutrition:

  • Food Science: Chemistry, Sensory Analysis, Quality Control, Food Engineering, Processing, etc.
  • Clinical Nutrition: Therapeutic Dietetics, Nutritional Interventions, Personalized Nutrition, Disease Management, etc.
  • Nutrition & Health: Epidemiology, Public Health Nutrition, Dietary Assessments, etc.
  • Food Technology: Safety, Preservation, Packaging, Novel Production Systems, etc.
