About the Journal

Vivrtih Journal of Food Science and Clinical Nutrition is an independent, researcher-led international journal.

Our mission is to bridge the gap between fundamental research and real-world applications by translating scientific discoveries into practical solutions for health, nutrition, and technology challenges.

Commitment to Quality

We adhere to the UGC Suggestive Parameters for peer-reviewed journals. Operating without commercial affiliation ensures our transparency and editorial integrity.

Scope & Focus Areas

We welcome high-quality work across the spectrum of food science and nutrition.

Food Science

Chemistry, Sensory Analysis, Quality Control, Engineering, and Processing.

Clinical Nutrition

Therapeutic Dietetics, Interventions, Personalized Nutrition, and Disease Management.

Nutrition & Health

Epidemiology, Public Health Nutrition, and Dietary Assessments.

Journal Standards

Publication Model

We utilize a Continuous Publication Model. Articles are published online immediately after acceptance to ensure your research reaches the community faster.

Inclusivity

We actively encourage submissions from students, early-career researchers, and young professionals alongside established scholars.
