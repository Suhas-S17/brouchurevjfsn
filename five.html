About the Journal

Vivrtih Journal of Food Science and Clinical Nutrition is an independent, researcher-led international journal. Our mission is to bridge the gap between fundamental research and real-world applications by translating scientific discoveries into practical solutions for health, nutrition, and technology challenges.

Commitment to Quality

We adhere to the UGC Suggestive Parameters for peer-reviewed journals. Operating without commercial affiliation ensures our transparency and editorial integrity.

International • Open-Access • Peer-Reviewed

Scope & Focus Areas

We welcome high-quality work across the spectrum of food science and nutrition:

  • Food Science: Chemistry, Sensory Analysis, Quality Control, Engineering.
  • Clinical Nutrition: Therapeutic Dietetics, Interventions, Disease Management.
  • Nutrition & Health: Epidemiology, Public Health, Dietary Assessments.
