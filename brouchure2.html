About the Journal

Vivrtih Journal of Food Science and Clinical Nutrition is an independent, researcher-led international journal. Our mission is to bridge the gap between fundamental research and real-world applications by translating scientific discoveries into practical solutions.

Commitment to Quality

We are dedicated to publishing high-quality, peer-reviewed scientific work. The journal operates without institutional or commercial affiliation, ensuring complete transparency and editorial integrity.

International • Open-Access • Peer-Reviewed

Scope & Focus Areas

We welcome submissions across the entire spectrum of food science and nutrition:

  • Food Science: Chemistry, Sensory Analysis, Quality Control, Food Engineering, Processing.
  • Clinical Nutrition: Therapeutic Dietetics, Nutritional Interventions, Personalized Nutrition, Disease Management.
  • Nutrition & Health: Epidemiology, Public Health Nutrition, Dietary Assessments.
  • Food Technology: Safety, Preservation, Packaging, Novel Production Systems.
