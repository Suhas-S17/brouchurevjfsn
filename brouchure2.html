About the Journal

Vivrtih Journal of Food Science and Clinical Nutrition is an independent, researcher-led international journal. We are dedicated to publishing high-quality, peer-reviewed scientific work that bridges the gap between fundamental research and real-world applications.

Our Mission

To translate scientific discoveries into practical solutions for health, nutrition, and technology challenges. We operate without institutional or commercial affiliation, ensuring complete transparency, editorial integrity, and unbiased decision-making throughout the publication process.

Commitment to Quality

We adhere to the "Suggestive Parameters for Peer Reviewed Journals" as notified by the University Grants Commission (UGC), India. Our continuous publication model ensures that articles are published online immediately upon acceptance, compiling into two formal issues per year.

Scope and Focus Areas

We invite researchers to contribute original work across the spectrum of nutrition and food technology. Our key focus areas include:

  • Food Science: Food Chemistry, Sensory Analysis, Quality Control, Food Engineering, and innovative processing techniques.
  • Clinical Nutrition: Therapeutic Dietetics, Nutritional Interventions, Personalized Nutrition, and Disease Management.
  • Nutrition & Health: Epidemiology, Public Health Nutrition, Dietary Assessments, and the impact of diet on human health.
  • Food Technology: Food Safety, Preservation Methods, Packaging, and Novel Food Production Systems.
