Scope & Focus Areas

We welcome submissions across the entire spectrum of food science and nutrition:

  • Food Science: Chemistry, Sensory Analysis, Quality Control, Food Engineering, Processing, etc.
  • Clinical Nutrition: Therapeutic Dietetics, Nutritional Interventions, Personalised Nutrition, Disease Management, etc.
  • Nutrition & Health: Epidemiology, Public Health Nutrition, Dietary Assessments, etc.
  • Food Technology: Safety, Preservation, Packaging, Novel Production Systems, etc.
Accepted Article Types

Original research, Review articles, Mini reviews, Short Reports, Case studies, Perspectives, Opinion Papers, Commentaries, and Letters to the Editor.

Inclusivity

While maintaining high academic standards, the journal encourages submissions from students, early-career researchers, and young professionals, supporting inclusivity and the professional growth of emerging scholars.

We welcome replication studies and rigorously conducted null findings. Every valid investigation, regardless of outcome, contributes valuable insights to the scientific community.
