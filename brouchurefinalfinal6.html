About the Journal

Vivrtih Journal of Food Science and Clinical Nutrition is an online-only, independent, researcher-led international journal. Our mission is to bridge the gap between fundamental research and practical applications by translating scientific discoveries into real-world solutions.

Commitment to Quality

We are dedicated to publishing high-quality, peer-reviewed scientific work. The journal operates without institutional or commercial affiliation, ensuring transparency, editorial independence, and adherence to ethical publishing standards.

Inclusivity

While maintaining high academic standards, the journal encourages submissions from students, early-career researchers, and young professionals, supporting inclusivity and the professional growth of emerging scholars.

For more information, kindly visit: www.vivrtihjournal.com

Scope & Focus Areas

We welcome submissions across the entire spectrum of food science and nutrition:

  • Food Science: Chemistry, Sensory Analysis, Quality Control, Food Engineering, Processing, etc.
  • Clinical Nutrition: Therapeutic Dietetics, Nutritional Interventions, Personalized Nutrition, Disease Management, etc.
  • Nutrition & Health: Epidemiology, Public Health Nutrition, Dietary Assessments, etc.
  • Food Technology: Safety, Preservation, Packaging, Novel Production Systems, etc.
