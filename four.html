About the Journal

Vivrtih Journal of Food Science and Clinical Nutrition is an independent, researcher-led international journal. We operate without institutional or commercial affiliation to ensure complete transparency and editorial integrity.

Our Mission

To bridge the gap between fundamental research and real-world applications. We translate scientific discoveries into practical solutions for health, nutrition, and technology challenges.

Inclusivity

We actively encourage submissions from students, early-career researchers, and young professionals alongside established scholars.

Scope & Focus Areas

We welcome high-quality original research and reviews across the spectrum of food science:

  • Food Science: Chemistry, Sensory Analysis, Quality Control, Engineering.
  • Clinical Nutrition: Therapeutic Dietetics, Interventions, Disease Management.
  • Nutrition & Health: Epidemiology, Public Health, Dietary Assessments.
  • Food Technology: Safety, Preservation, Packaging, Novel Systems.
